The front cover of the book shows a light blue sphere cutaway so that the hollow center is visible. The walls of the sphere are light blue on their thin inner and thicker outer boundaries with a gray middle region. Many red and white molecules are inside and outside and a larger horizontal molecule in the center has red, white, and pink pieces. A logo of Macmillan learning is at the bottom right corner.

The title reads, Lehninger Principles of Biochemistry (eighth edition), followed by the names of the authors, David L. Nelson and Michael M. Cox.
